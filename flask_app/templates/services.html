Services Offered

Event Catering:

Mohican's Top Notch Catering specializes in providing exceptional catering services for a variety of events, ranging from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations. Our off-site and on-site catering options offer personalized menus, themed presentations, and an infusion of rustic elegance into every culinary experience.

Customized Gift Baskets:

We pride ourselves on curating personalized gift baskets tailored to suit various occasions. Our gift baskets feature an array of delectable items, carefully selected to showcase the finest local produce and artisanal treats. From charcuterie selections to specialty items like pinwheels and chocolate-covered strawberries, each basket is crafted to delight the recipient.

Charcuterie and Fresh Produce Pick-Up:

For those seeking a taste of sophistication and freshness, we offer the convenience of in-store pick-up for charcuterie boards, fruit baskets/trays, and other specialty items. Customers can select from our range of carefully curated offerings or place customized orders for their specific preferences.

Food Allergies and Freshness:

At Mohican's Top Notch Catering, we prioritize the well-being of our clients by paying meticulous attention to food allergies and maintaining the utmost freshness in our offerings. Our culinary team takes special care to accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences, ensuring each dish is crafted with the highest quality, locally sourced ingredients.

Commitment to Quality

We are committed to delivering not just food but an experience. Our dedication to sourcing fresh, local produce and our unwavering attention to detail in every aspect of our service set us apart. Customers can trust that their catering, gift baskets, or pick-up items from Mohican's Top Notch Catering are crafted with care and dedication
